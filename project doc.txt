# 🛡️ Guardian AI - Project Development Blueprint

## Project Overview

### Project Name

Guardian AI

### Tagline

An AI-powered multi-agent security assistant that protects users from scams, phishing, fake documents, fraudulent websites, and digital threats using multimodal reasoning.

### Problem Statement

Digital fraud is becoming increasingly sophisticated. Most users struggle to identify phishing websites, fake job offers, forged documents, manipulated screenshots, and online scams. Existing solutions often detect only one type of threat and fail to provide clear reasoning behind their decisions.

### Goal

Build a multi-agent AI system capable of analyzing text, images, documents, and screenshots to detect digital threats, explain the reasoning behind each decision, and recommend appropriate actions.

### Primary Objectives

* Detect phishing attempts.
* Analyze suspicious documents.
* Verify screenshots and images.
* Evaluate scam messages.
* Provide transparent AI reasoning.
* Demonstrate collaborative multi-agent intelligence.
